Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the process.

What is the delivery time?
For the Starter and Business websites, I typically deliver within 3-4 working days. The Premium Custom websites may take around 7-10 days depending on the complexity of functionality integrations such as payments and custom admin panel setup.
What is the payment process?
I usually ask for a 50% upfront payment to start working on the wireframes and coding. The remaining 50% is paid after completing the website and just before launching it live on your domain.
Do you provide domain and hosting?
No, the server and domain (URL) costs are paid directly to the registrar providers (like Hostinger, GoDaddy). However, I will happily guide you step-by-step through the process or handle the setup totally free of charge on your behalf.
What about after-sales support details?
Each package comes with included post-launch support. I'll fix any bugs free of charge. Long-term maintenance beyond that period can be done seamlessly under a small monthly retainer.
